Hori 64 TrackMania Controls
Start - Pause
C UP - Save Replay
C DOWN - Respawn
C LEFT - Give Up
C RIGHT - Show/Hide Ghosts
Z - Horn
R - Change Camera (Camera 3)
L - Look Behind (Camera 1)
A - Gas
B - Brake
DPad RIGHT - Show/Hide Scores
DPad LEFT - Secondary respawn (TM2020 ONLY)

8BitDo SN30 TrackMania Controls
Start - Pause
X - Respawn
Select - Give Up
A - Horn
R - Change Camera (Camera 3)
L - Look Behind (Camera 1 | Secondary Respawn)
B - Gas
Y - Brake
DPad RIGHT - Steer Right
DPad LEFT - Steer Left
